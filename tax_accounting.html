    Tax & Accounting

    Our specialist tax team provide tailored tax services ensuring our clients' requirements are met with regards to tax compliance, tax planning, asset and wealth protection, all whilst legitimately minimising taxes.

    In the complex landscape of tax compliance, it pays to have the best support and advice. Our extensive knowledge in income tax legislation and services means we will keep you up to date whilst also achieving the best outcome. This will save you time, effort and money and will ensure that you can keep focussing on your goals whilst we look after the detail.

    We have been providing taxation services for over 30 years. Originally established as Athans & Taylor Joondalup, our firm has grown and evolved to who we are today as A&T Accountants and Advisers. Our business tax accountants are constantly monitoring changes to Australia's tax legislation to ensure new compliance obligations are met and efficient tax strategies are adopted.

    Tax and Accounting services we offer our clients:

    • Individual tax returns, including sole traders, investment properties, share traders
    • Company, trusts and partnerships
    • GST/Business Activity Statements (BAS)/Instalment Activity Statements (IAS)
    • Capital Gains Tax (CGT)
    • Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT)
    • Strategic tax planning
